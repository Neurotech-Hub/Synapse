Minimum useful record

A title and source URL are enough to start. Add sponsor, deadline, amount, effort, and tags as available; most fields can stay blank for messy sources.

Public fields

Public summary, eligibility, amount, deadline, effort, and tags may appear on Funding Radar after review. Private summary and notes are admin-only.

Effort override

Set effort manually when you know the mechanism better than the heuristic. Confidence is set as reviewed when the label or rationale changes here.
