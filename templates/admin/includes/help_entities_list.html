People, organizations, places

Each source has at most one person or organization owner (people can also carry an org membership). That pairing drives persona evidence paths for lead reports and Hub corpus membership.

Openness score

The Openness score (0–1) is LLM-inferred from each person's publication record and activity signals. It reflects how openly their work pattern invites external collaboration — not whether they are a good NTH lead. A separate fit score on each lead report uses the NTH scoring rubric for that judgment.
